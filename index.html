Biography

Nicole is a creative artist and marketing professional committed to assessing customer needs and designing customized solutions to meet their sales and marketing needs. She has a Bachelor of Arts in Animation from Collins College in Tempe, AZ. She is currently in the process of acquiring a Bachelor of Science in Web Development from Bellevue University in Bellevue, NE. Nicole is a perfectionist who has worked on many different styles of projects from invitations to corporate marketing material. Now she is learning to create websites not only using front end development but as well as back end development. She is a single mom, going to school and working fulltime. She loves being creative both at work and at home while enjoying time with her family.
